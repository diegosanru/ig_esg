Health and Safety at Industrial Gate

Safe Work Environment
Prevention and Regulatory Compliance
Safety Training
Promotion of Comprehensive Health
Evaluation and Continuous Improvement

At Industrial Gate, we prioritize the physical integrity and well-being of all our employees. We implement clear protocols for prevention and incident response, ensuring that our operations take place in a safe and controlled environment.

We ensure that all our labor practices comply with current regulations regarding health and safety. This includes continuous risk assessment and the adoption of preventive measures to minimize any potential incident.

We foster a preventive culture through periodic training programs, where our staff acquires practical knowledge to act safely in their work area and respond appropriately in emergency situations.

In addition to physical safety, we promote comprehensive well-being through initiatives that encourage healthy habits, emotional care, and work-life balance.

We maintain a monitoring and evaluation system that allows us to identify opportunities for improvement in our health and safety policies and practices, ensuring their effectiveness and constant updating.
