Energy Performance

At Industrial Gate, we implement strategies focused on optimizing energy use in our operations, prioritizing efficiency and the adoption of technologies that reduce our consumption and associated emissions.

Our developments incorporate high-efficiency LED lighting systems, low-energy equipment, and automation solutions that allow for more precise resource control.

Likewise, we continuously monitor key energy performance indicators (KPIs), which allows us to identify areas for improvement, reduce operating costs, and minimize our environmental footprint, in line with criteria established by certifications such as EDGE and LEED.

Water Performance

Aware of the importance of water as an essential resource, we have adopted measures to responsibly manage its consumption in our facilities.

In our industrial developments, we incorporate rainwater harvesting and utilization systems, water-saving fixtures, and technologies that allow for the reuse of the resource for irrigation and other non-potable uses.

Regular monitoring of our consumption helps us implement continuous improvements, ensuring that our operations maintain a responsible water balance and contribute to Sustainable Development Goal 6 (Clean Water and Sanitation).

Emissions

Reducing greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions is a priority in our environmental strategy.

At Industrial Gate, we periodically measure our carbon footprint, considering scopes 1 and 2, with the goal of establishing clear reduction targets and adopting concrete measures to achieve them.

Our actions include the use of materials with lower environmental impact, the promotion of clean energy, and the optimization of logistics processes to reduce travel and energy consumption.

With these actions, we advance towards cleaner operations aligned with global commitments to climate change mitigation.

Waste Management

Responsible waste management is a key pillar of our sustainability strategy. We promote responsible handling practices, such as proper waste disposal and the identification of management and recovery options for each type of waste. These actions have been promoted both internally and administratively, as well as with our tenants and suppliers through communications and training.

In line with continuous improvement, we will begin formal waste measurement at our properties. This step will allow us to establish baselines and design more effective management strategies in the short and medium term.
